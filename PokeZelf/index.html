Welcome to PokeZelf!

Guides for Gen 4/5 games and Wiimmfi!

Nintendo WFC was shutted down by GameSpy, on 20th May 2014. Since this date, you're not supposed to play DS and Wii games online. Then, Wiimmfi opened their servers. Wiimmfi uses the DNS exploit for DS and Wii games. Now, you just have to change the DNS in your DS/Wii Wifi settings, and then you will be able to play online.

Most of DS and Wii games are supported by Wiimmfi. Check the list of all games supported by Wiimmfi here. PokeZelf has (or will have) breeding guides, strategy guides, online Pokedex... and also tips for Pokemon Diamond, Pearl, Platinum, HeartGold, SoulSilver, Black, White, Black 2, White 2, and Pokemon Battle Revolution (Wii).

Wiimmfi has a really big community, specifically in Mario Kart (Wii & DS) and Pokemon. You can check which Pokemon is in the GTS here, and check who is connected on each game here. Most of people play Mario Kart Wii, and you will have to send your friend code to the person who you wanna play with.
